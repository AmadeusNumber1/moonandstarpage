About

Mati Moon & Jenny Star are music industry professionals who have extended their talents to brands as artists — both on stage and behind the scenes.

Their music has been heard on the radio and featured in television and film. They have toured the world separately and together, and bring a timeless, Old Hollywood charm to every private event, cocktail hour, and celebration.

Meeting in Berlin in the summer of 2012, they made Los Angeles their home in 2015. By day they write and produce songs for various artists and compose for TV and film. By night, their lively renditions of classic hits and jazz standards create an atmosphere of pure enjoyment and unforgettable entertainment.

Mati Moon

Multi-instrumentalist, composer, producer, singer, and multimedia artist. Born in Tel Aviv, raised in Germany. Released a solo album on Warner Music in 2015. His eclectic, international upbringing infuses every performance with a rare depth and warmth.

Jenny Star

Los Angeles-based singer, writer, and vocal producer. One of the industry's most in-demand session and live singers, having worked across New York, London, Berlin, and LA with artists including Jennifer Lopez, Elton John, Britney Spears, Celine Dion, and many more.
